You will need the following ingredients to make a great Guacomole!!

  2. 1 lemon
  3. 1 lime
  4. handful of cilantro
  5. 1 serrano Chile
  6. 1 1/2 large tomato or 2 medium tomato
  7. half of a red onion
  8. salt
  9. pepper
  10. (Optional Garlic salt instead of regular)
